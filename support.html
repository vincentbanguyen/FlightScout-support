How it works

What is FlightScout?

FlightScout monitors flight prices on the routes you care about and alerts you when a deal drops significantly below the historical average — so you never miss a cheap flight.

How do watch pairs work?

A watch pair is a route you want to track (e.g., LAX to Tokyo). You set your origin, destination, travel dates, and preferences like round trip or nonstop. FlightScout continuously checks prices and notifies you when it finds a deal.

What counts as a "deal"?

A deal is a flight price that's at least 30% below the historical average for that route. The bigger the drop, the better the deal — you'll see the exact percentage on each deal card.

Search & dates

What is flexible date search?

Instead of picking exact dates, you specify a travel window (e.g., anytime in June) and a trip duration (e.g., 5 nights). FlightScout searches across multiple departure dates within your window to find the cheapest combination.

How often are prices checked?

FlightScout checks your routes periodically in the background. You can also pull to refresh on the feed to trigger an on-demand search for the latest prices.

Alerts & notifications

How do deal alerts work?

When FlightScout finds a price that meets your alert threshold (default 30% below average), it sends you a push notification. You can customize the alert threshold per route in Settings.

Why am I not getting notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in the app's Settings and in iOS Settings > FlightScout > Notifications. Deal alerts require an active Pro subscription.

Booking

Can I book flights in the app?

FlightScout does not handle bookings directly. When you find a deal you like, tap it to open the flight details and use the deep link to complete your booking on the airline or travel site.

Troubleshooting

My feed says "no deals found."

This means current prices are close to or above the historical average for your routes. Keep your watch pairs active — you'll be notified as soon as a price drops into deal territory.

Is my data private?

Your watch pairs and preferences are stored securely. Flight data is not tied to your identity. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
